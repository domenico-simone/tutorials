    Home¶

    Welcome!

    This website is a collection of Bioinformatics tutorials that I've accumulated over the years, while teaching a bioinformatics course at the Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences and during various bioinformatics workshops around the globe.

    Feel free to follow them online, or to use and modify them for your own teaching.
